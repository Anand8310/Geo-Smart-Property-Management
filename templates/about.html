About Geo-Smart PMS

Welcome to Geo-Smart PMS, your all-in-one platform for modern property management.

Our mission is to solve the biggest challenges in the real estate market by integrating advanced geospatial technology with a comprehensive management system. We aim to provide a transparent, efficient, and data-driven experience for property owners, tenants, and service vendors.

Our Platform

We have built a sophisticated, multi-faceted application that includes:

  • Property Marketplace: An interactive map with advanced filters for tenants to discover properties and see nearby points of interest.
  • Property Management System: A full suite of tools for owners and tenants to manage leases, invoices, and documents.
  • Vendor Marketplace: A complete workflow for owners to hire and manage approved vendors for maintenance tasks.
  • Neighborhood Insights Hub: A powerful analytics dashboard to analyze property trends and market data in real-time.
  • Automated Onboarding System: A professional workflow for tenants to apply for properties and for owners to approve or reject them.

This project was built to demonstrate the power of integrating a GIS backend (PostgreSQL with PostGIS) with a modern web framework (Python/Django) to solve complex, real-world problems.
